Game Rules & Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to Western Frontier! Start by selecting a city from the world map. Each city has different buildings and activities. You begin at level 57 in Desert Mirage City.

Movement

Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character around the city. The camera will follow your character as you explore.

Interactions

When you approach a building, an interaction prompt will appear. Press E to enter the building and play games or access services.

Buildings

• Golden Saloon: Play poker and other card games
• Horseshoe Arena: Participate in tournaments and battles
• Poker Shack: High-stakes poker games
• General Store: Buy supplies and equipment
• Bank: Deposit and withdraw your gold

Leveling Up

As you play games and complete activities, you'll gain experience and level up. Higher levels unlock new cities with more challenges and rewards.

Currency

• Gold: The main currency used for transactions
• Gems: Premium currency for special items and services

Map Navigation

The minimap in the bottom left shows your current position in the city. Use the hamburger menu in the top right to view your stats and navigate back to the map.

NPC Interactions

Click on NPCs to talk with them. They may provide useful information, quests, or special items.
